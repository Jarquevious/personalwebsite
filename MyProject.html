Developing A technical solution to the tangible vaccine card delimma

WHY VAXME EXISTS Coronavirus has undoubtedly changed life as we know it, so we adapted. When the CDC began administering the Covid-19 Vaccine, they left only a single method of verifying that vaccination - a 4" X 3" paper card with your vaccine info hand-written on it. Living in the 21st century, and having our phones within reach at all times enabled the potential for digital verification to become a better alternative. With Vaxme, we provide a simple to use app integrated with a biometric screening that allows people to easily verify that they have been vaccinated against the virus in order to gain entry into events, venues, restaurants, airports and more.
